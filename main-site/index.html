About Canine Relational Coaching

Transform your dog's behavior with expert coaching. Many of the behaviors we label as nuisances or annoying, such as barking, reactivity, leash-pulling, toileting inside, jumping, separation anxiety, can be looked to as signs of a deeper misunderstanding between humans and their dogs. My work focuses on expanding perspective: helping people see behavior not as defiance, but as communication.

My focus is on the relational dynamic as a whole. Each individual dog brings their own history, personality, and breed characteristics. Each human brings their own history, personality, and expectations. Instead of relying on quick fixes, I guide clients to lean into curiosity to discover the root of the problem. Through empathetic connection and clear, science-based explanation, I help dog-human teams build consistency, confidence, and genuine understanding.

The result is more than better manners, it's a transformed relationship. By learning to interpret differently, you and your dog will gain stronger communication, deeper trust, and the tools to navigate new challenges together.
